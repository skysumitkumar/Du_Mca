1. At 6'o a clock ticks 6 times.
    The time between first and last ticks is 30 seconds.
    How long does it tick at 12'o clock.

Ans: 66 sec.                 (2 marks) 


2. Three friends divided some bullets equally.
    After all of them shot 4 bullets the total number of bullets remaining is equal to the 
bullets each had after division.
    Find the original number divided. 

Ans: 18                         (2 marks)

Initially   .    x          x        x 
Now          x-4       x-4     x-4 
Equation is   3x-12 = x
