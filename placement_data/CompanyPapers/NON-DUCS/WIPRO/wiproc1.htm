T - Question pattern - 01                   Comments from Candidates   



I have cleared selection process in WIPRO's ME/MTECH freshers recruitment(in VLSI) conducted on April 29, 2004. In this regard I would like to share my journey from test to selection process with some of my group members. Also I would give some tips for ME/MTECH Freshers recruitment conducted by WIPRO in Embedded Systems. 

In the written test conducted on April 29, 2004...the test had three sections namely VERBAL (10), ANALYTICAL (10), TECHNICAL(30) with total of 50 Marks. For VERBAL and ANALYTICAL sections, I brushed the Barron's GRE Book and fortunately the paper was set on that format and the question were quite easy. Coming to Technical section, since the job was in VLSI, I prepared for DIGITAL CIRCUITS, LOGIC DESIGN and basic concepts in VLSI. Though there were some questions from these subjects...some questions also appeared from 
Communications and Linear Circuits. Thankfully, no questions from Electromagnetic waves and other such related topics. There were no negative marking and test was for 90 minutes. So ample of time.

For the interview, the main emphasis was on how perfect and confident I am in Digital and Logic Design. Most of the questions are related to Clock Timing, Setup and Hold time etc. Not much of emphasis was on Project work done. But they asked the subject which I had in my ME and all the questions were asked from those subjects.In HR, they asked me about my earliest joining date and some basic questions like Describe Yourself, how comfortable r u with Bond, ur preferred work locations, family members etc.

Regarding WIPRO's next recruitment (if anyone doesnot know regarding this), they are recruiting ME/MTECH freshers in Embedded Systems for ECE and CS branches. The last date for submission is 9th May. You can submit from Wipro's website.

Well for the written test, just prepare BARRON's GRE book but dont study as u study for GRE exams. Just brush up few words and practice the Model tests given in the book. Regarding technical section... prepare for the subjects related to this field and dont unnecessarily study irrelevant subjects. For the interview, prepare for 3 relevant subjects and they will ask most from these subjects. Make sure that u do ur technical interview well bcoz HR interview is just a formality. So prepare well for TEST and TECHNICAL interview. Just be relaxed and prepare with hard work. Assess my experience and You can definitely succeed.
