BIRLASOFT PATTERN - 10 JAN 2006 - CHENNAI
Hi Chetanaites
company:birlasoft
place:chennai
date 10 jan
type:freshers(campus)

the paper had 70-80 questions to be completed in 1 hr 30 min.
the test had 20 ques on technical..from c,c++(quest here were mostly on tryin to find the output of the
given program). www.chetanasinterview.com
there were ques on sql,
the next was an english section,it had
sentence correction
jumbled sentences
critical reasoning
2-3 passages
2 sets of logical reasonig questions
 and some questions on psychometric...
no questions from quantitative aptitude
the next was GD AND INTERVIEW..my interview panel
asked questions more on real time problems (situations)
and asked me to devise a soltn strategy....
on the whole if u clear the first round u r thru..
