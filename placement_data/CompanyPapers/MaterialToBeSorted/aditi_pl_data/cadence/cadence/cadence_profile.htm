Profile

In 1997, Cadance Corporation was established with the goal of providing comprehensive Information Technology (IT) solutions for small to medium sized companies. Towards that end, we have aligned ourselves with several of the "best of breed" solution providers, including IBM, Lotus, Hewlett Packard, and Cisco. These alliances allow Cadance Corporation to provide superior, cost effective solutions in a timely and efficient manner.
