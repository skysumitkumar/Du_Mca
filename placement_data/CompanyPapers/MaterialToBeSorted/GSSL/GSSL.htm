  • Profile

Geometric Software Solutions is a company specializing in providing software technology and development services to CAD/CAM/CAE/PDM vendors world-wide. Geometric Software offers expertise in solid and surface modeling, feature recognition, data translators, ACIS and Parasolid geometry kernels, PDM, nesting, and machining.

Geometric was established in 1994. Prior to that, the activities were a part of a division of Godrej & Boyce Mfg. Co. Ltd. Geometric is organized into Component Software, Customer Services, OEM Applications and Plug-in Applications.

For more information, visit the company's website at www.geometricsoftware.com
