Paper of Infosys at IIT Bombay on 3rd August 2002.

1. The price of a commodity increases 25% every odd year and decreses 20%
every even year. COunting from today what is the price after 10 years.
Ans: No change.

2. A and B are 36 kms apart. The start simultaneously and plan to meet on
cycles exactly midway of the distance. Both move with a velcity 6 km/s. A
bird is sitting on the head of A and as A and B starts cycling it flies
towards B. When the bird reaches B it immediately turns back and moves
towards A. It continues flying from A to B and vice versa till A and B
meet. The velocity of the bird is 18 km/hr. What distance did the bird
travel.

Ans: 54 Km.

3. I move from a train from Mumbai to Chandigarh by ABCD express. The
train starts at 0900 hours and reaches Chandigarh 3 days later at
0930. Exactly similar is the schedule of the train from Chandigarh to
Mumbai again named ABCD express. How may trains with similar names will i
meet en route.

Ans: 7

4. I have a string 20 cms long. I cut in two parts. Find the probability
that:

a. The area of the largest is more than 9 cm2.
b. The sum of the areas is more than equal to 20.25 cm2.

a. 4/5
b. 1/5 (Verify the answers/ Questions)

5. My house is between numbers 1 to 82. A fellow mathematician asks my my
house number in the three questions.

a. Is your house number lies in 1 to 41
b. Is your house number divisible by 4
c. Is it a perfect square.

I wont tell you what answers I gave but the guy waas able to figure out my
house number.... Now its your turn to do the same

Ans: 64
