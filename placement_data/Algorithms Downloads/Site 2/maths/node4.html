Foreword

This course1 studies computer algorithms, considering their construction, validation and effectiveness. After a general introduction to the subject a number of specific topics will be covered. These may include: the problem of sorting data sets into order, the theory of formal grammars and problems such as the parsing of arithmetic expressions, the construction and use of pseudorandom numbers.
