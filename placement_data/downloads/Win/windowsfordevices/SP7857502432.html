Vibren Technologies
Macromedia Flash Player for embedded devices
Vibren Technologies, a wholly-owned by NEC System Technologies of Japan and NEC Solutions America, is the exclusive authorized distributor of Macromedia Flash Player for embedded devices in North America.

Vibren provides embedded device OEMs with the ability to play Macromedia Flash content on their device. Macromedia Flash Player is the optimum client for viewing Macromedia Flash content on embedded devices. Macromedia Flash is used by over one million designers and developers worldwide to integrate video, text, audio, and graphics into rich internet applications.

Macromedia Flash is the number one software platform in the world. Based on its flexible architecture and small footprint, Macromedia Flash Player is readily ported to a wide array of embedded devices. Vibren’s engineers have been working with Macromedia Flash Player since version 4 and are experts in optimizing the player for maximum performance on diverse embedded platforms.

Our experienced and focused engineering team specializes in porting Macromedia Flash Player to your product and licensing its distribution. Contact us for more information.
