Puzzle 6

Imagine you have one cup of tea and one cup of coffee. Place a spoonful of the tea into the coffee and then place a spoonful of the tea/coffee mixture back into the tea. Does the cup of coffee now contain more tea or the cup of tea more coffee?

   Ref: ZGED

Surprisingly, they are actually the same. If we consider a full cup to be 100 units, and the spoonful to be 10 units. We place 10 tea into the coffee cup, which now contains 100 coffee and 10 tea, we place 10 of this 100/10 mix into the tea. This 10 contains 100/11 of coffee and 10/11 of tea. So the coffee cup is still left with 100/11 of tea - which is the same amount of coffee we put into the tea cup.
