Neptune Funds
Who manages the moneymaking Neptune Mutual Funds?
November 3, 2003
By clue 1, Linda's fund is located one floor above Mr. (clue 8) Stock's, whose office is one floor higher than that of the Neptune Small Cap Fund; by clue 10, the Asia Fund's office is one floor above Carol's office, which is one floor higher than Mr. (clue 2) Sellers' office. The two clues do not overlap completely, since Carol isn't Mr. Stock. Linda can't be Mr. Sellers. The only other possible overlap would be that Stock manages the Neptune Asia Fund--no (8). Therefore, all six offices are given between clues 1 and 10, so that either Linda or the Asia Fund has the 6th floor office with the other of the two then being on the 3rd floor. If the Asia Fund office were on the 6th floor, by clue 10, Carol's would be on the 5th and Mr. Sellers' on the 4th; while by clue 1, Linda would have the 3rd floor suite, with Mr. Stock on the 2nd and the Small Cap Fund on the 1st. By clue 4, Margaret would manage the Asia Fund and Carol the Neptune Municipals Fund. Since Mr. Sellers doesn't manage the Neptune Technology Fund (2), by clue 7, Mr. Stock would, with Gaines then the Small Cap manager. Carol would be Bond, and Sellers would manage the Neptune Value Fund (5). By elimination, Linda's fund would be Neptune Large Cap. Linda's surname then would be Cash (9). However, Cash's office isn't on the 3rd floor (11), a conflict. Therefore, the Asia Fund office isn't on the 6th floor; Linda's is. By clue 1, Mr. Stock's office is on the 5th and Neptune Small Cap on the 4th floor; while by clue 10, the Asia Fund is located on the 3rd, Carol's office on the 2nd, and Mr. Sellers' fund on the 1st floor. By clue 4, Margaret manages the Neptune Asia Fund and Carol Neptune Municipals. Mr. Stock manages Neptune Technology, and Gaines the Small Cap Fund (7). By clue 5, Carol is Bond; and Sellers runs the Neptune Value Fund. By elimination, Linda oversees the Neptune Large Cap Fund. Then Linda's surname is Cash and Margaret's Money (9). By clue 6, John is Mr. Stock. Adam is Sellers and Robert Gaines (3). In sum, the six Neptune Funds are located on floors in the Neptune Building as follows:

  • 6th - Neptune Large Cap, Linda Cash
  • 5th - Neptune Technology, John Stock
  • 4th - Neptune Small Cap, Robert Gaines
  • 3rd - Neptune Asia, Margaret Money
  • 2nd - Neptune Municipals, Carol Bond
  • 1st - Neptune Value, Adam Sellers
