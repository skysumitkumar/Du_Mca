CADENCE DESIGN SYSTEMS (INDIA) PRIVATE LIMITED

PROFILE

Cadence Design Systems (India) Private Limited is a fully owned subsidiary of Cadence Design Systems Inc. and has been in operation since 1987 in the Noida Export Processing Zone, near New Delhi. The India Center is the largest R&D site of Cadence outside the United States and employs over 130 professionals.

Cadence India's products complement the parent company's product range. Its traditional strengths have been in the areas of Hardware Description Languages (both VHDL and Verilog) and tools associated with these languages.

Cadence develops some of the World's most sophisticated software for Electronics Design Automation (EDA) and provides design & consulting services which give customers a distinct competitive edge by improving time to market, quality and productivity. Cadence India has the distinction of being Cadence's first R&D site to receive the prestigious ISO 9001/TickIT certification. Cadence India has representation at international forums like the IEEE Timing subcommittee and the Synthesis Interoperability Working Group (SIWG) of VHDL International. Cadence India played a key role in promoting the VLSI Society of India.
