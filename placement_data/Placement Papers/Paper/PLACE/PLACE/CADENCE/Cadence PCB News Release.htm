ECAD Technologies Adopts Cadence PCB Design Solution


 
BANGALORE, India — October 15, 2001 — Cadence Design Systems, Inc. (NYSE: CDN), the world’s leading supplier of electronic design products and services, today announced that ECAD Technologies Ltd., Bangalore, India, has deployed Cadence® high-speed printed circuit board (PCB) design solutions to help automate its layout process for high-speed boards.

ECAD Technologies, a company with core competence in the field of design engineering for electronic systems, has chosen Cadence PCB Design Expert and SPECCTRAQuest™ Signal Integrity (SI) Expert to use in designing its high-performance products. The PCB Design environment includes Allegro® Expert, Constraint Manager, PCB Librarian, Concept® HDL, and SPECCTRA® Expert.

Faced with increasingly complex PCB designs, ECAD began evaluating tools that would improve its ability to manage the technological challenges inherent in these products. The company assessed tools from a variety of EDA vendors by having evaluation teams use them to design a high-performance automotive PCB. Ensuring signal integrity was a key success criterion, although a tool’s capability to handle all aspects of the design process was considered. Enabling team-based design entry, capturing large complex designs easily, and having an integrated capture application and an effective re-use support system were all taken into account.

"As a part of ECAD’s expansion plan, we decided to thoroughly evaluate and purchase a very robust high-end PCB design and high-speed simulation system," said S. L. N. Murthy, chairman and managing director, ECAD Technologies Ltd. "Cadence proved that it has a scalable solution that is capable of addressing our present and future needs."

"ECAD is posed with the challenge of reducing time-to-market and increasing the productivity of its high-speed PCB design teams. The integrated Cadence PCB design environment will help them to achieve this goal," said Himanshu Singh, country manager of Cadence Design Systems (I) Pvt. Ltd. "ECAD’s choice of SPECCTRAQuest SI Expert underscores the product’s position in the industry as an unparalleled solution to signal integrity and constraint management issues."

According to Singh, ECAD Technologies is representative of a vital electronic design industry in India. He believes Indian companies will continue to grow and to successfully compete in global PCB and other electronics design markets.

About Cadence
Cadence is the largest supplier of electronic design automation products, methodology services, and design services. Cadence solutions are used to accelerate and manage the design of semiconductors, computer systems, networking and telecommunications equipment, consumer electronics, and a variety of other electronics-based products. With approximately 5,700 employees and year 2000 revenues of nearly $1.3 billion, Cadence has sales offices, design centers, and research facilities around the world. The company is headquartered in San Jose, Calif., and traded on the New York Stock Exchange under the symbol CDN. More information about the company, its products, and services is available at http://www.cadence.com/.

About ECAD Technologies Ltd.
ECAD Technologies Ltd. is a technology savvy company, incorporated in 1997. The company’s core competence is in the field of design engineering for electronic systems using Computer Aided Engineering tools (CAD). The company began its operations as a CAD design service bureau.

It has to its credit a satisfied clientele list which reads like a "who’s who" in electronics in India. The company has grown in its operations over the past two years, primarily based on the customer confidence and company’s commitment to quality of service. ECAD Technologies addresses design realisation challenges of electronic system design engineers. The company recognised as an EDA Technology Service Provider (TSP) is located at Bangalore - the Silicon Valley of India. The company is a TSP for both on-site and off-site projects. It provides engineering consultancy in areas like signal integrity, thermal analysis and EMC engineering solutions for circuit boards used in high performance systems. http://www.ecadtech.com/.
