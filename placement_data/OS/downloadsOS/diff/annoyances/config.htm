    The text you type for your header and footer will appear at the beginning and end, respectively, of every message you post at Annoyances.org. Each field can be a maximum of 255 characters and can include HTML codes.

    Tip: make sure to close any formatting codes you open. For instance, you can make your entire message blue by placing <font color=blue> in the header and a corresponding </font> in the footer.

    Tip: to place a small image (avatar) in your signature, use this code (with your own URL):
    <img src="http://www.your_server.com/image.jpg">
