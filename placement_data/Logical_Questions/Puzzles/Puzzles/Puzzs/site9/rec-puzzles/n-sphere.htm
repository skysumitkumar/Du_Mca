Solution to the /induction/n-sphere problem

Select three points a, b, and c, randomly with respect to the surface of an n-sphere. These three points determine a fourth, x, which is the intersection of the sphere with the axis perpendicular to the abc plane. (Choose the pole nearest the plane.) I could have, just as easily, selected x, a distance d from x, and three points d units away from x. The distribution of d is not uniform, but that's ok. For every x and d, the three points abc form an acute triangle with probability p[n-1]. By induction, p[n] = 1/4.
