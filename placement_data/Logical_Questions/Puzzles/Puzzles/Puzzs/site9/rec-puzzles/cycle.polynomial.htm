Solution to the /geometry/cycle.polynomial problem

For future reference, here are the cycle polynomials for the five platonic solids (and I threw in the tesseract for good measure). Most combinatoric coloring problems are simple plug-ins to these polynomials. For details, see any book on combinatorics that presents Polya counting theory.
  • tetrahedron: (x1^4+3x2^2+8x1*x3)/12
  • cube: (x1^6+6x2^3+3x1^2*x2^2+8x3^2+6x1^2*x4)/24
  • octahedron: (x1^8+9x2^4+8x1^2*x3^2+6x4^2)/24
  • dodecahedron: (x1^12+15x2^6+20x3^4+24x1^2*x5^2)/60
  • icosahedron: (x1^20+15x2^10+20x1^2*x3^6+24x5^4)/60
  • tesseract: (32x6^4+x2^12+48x8^3+x1^24+24x1^2*x2^11+12x2^2*x4^5+32x3^8+12x4^6 +18x1^4*x2^10+12x1^4*x4^5)/192
