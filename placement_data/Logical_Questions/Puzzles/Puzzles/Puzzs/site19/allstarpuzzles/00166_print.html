Dr. Bill, with help from the studio audience, gave each woman, including Meg, his usual down-home advice on how to solve the problem. Given the clues below, can you solve the Logic Problem of last week's Dr. Bill Show lineup: the full name of the guest and her issue for each day Monday-Friday?
