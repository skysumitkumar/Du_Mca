Star Lookalikes
Which high schooler looks most like a star?
April 18, 2005
Between clues 1 and 5, in each of which three different Star Lookalike contestants are named, there must be some overlap: either Wendy placed 1st with Angela 3rd; Wendy placed 1st or 2nd with Angela then one place back at 2nd or 3rd; Angela placed 1st with Wendy 3rd; or Angela placed 1st or 2nd with Wendy then one place back in 2nd or 3rd. If Wendy had won the contest with Angela finishing 3rd, combining clues 1 and 5 would put the Britney Spears lookalike in 2nd, Angela Greenlee in 3rd, the Christina Aguilera lookalike in 4th, and Miss Grayson in 5th place. Since Tina isn't the Christina Aguilera lookalike (clue 4), by clue 7, she would be the Britney Spears lookalike and Wendy would be the Hilary Duff lookalike. However, since Miss Grayson didn't enter as the Mary-Kate Olsen lookalike (8), clue 3 cannot work. If Wendy had finished one position ahead of Angela in the judging, combining clues 1 and 5 would have Wendy, Britney Spears lookalike Wendy, Christina Aguilera lookalike Miss Greenlee, and Miss Grayson finishing in consecutive order in the contest. By clue 7, Miss Grayson would be the Hilary Duff lookalike, with Tina placing 5th in the event. Miss Grayson would be Erin (3), with Faith, by elimination, Miss Greenlee--no (2). So, Wendy did not finish ahead of Angela in the event. If Angela had won the contest with Wendy 3rd, combining clues 1 and 5 would give Angela in 1st, the Christina Aguilera lookalike in 2nd, Wendy Grayson in 3rd, the Britney Spears lookalike in 4th, and Miss Greenlee in 5th place. Since Tina isn't the one who entered as Christina Aguilera (4), by clue 7, Tina would be the Britney Spears lookalike and Wendy would have entered as the Hilary Duff lookalike. However, there is no way for clue 3 to work given this arrangement. Therefore, Angela placed one position ahead of Wendy, as Christina Aguilera, in the judging, followed in order by Miss Grayson as Britney Spears and Miss Greenlee. By clue 7, Miss Greenlee is the Hilary Duff lookalike; and Tina finished in 5th place in the event. Angela thus was 1st. By clue 3, Miss Greenlee is Erin; and Tina appeared as Mary-Kate Olsen. By elimination, Faith is Miss Grayson, and Angela won by looking like Lindsay Lohan. Angela's surname is Browner (6). Tina is Miss Blackmun and Wendy Miss Whitehurst (4). In sum, the results of the Star Lookalike Contest are

  • 1st - Lindsay Lohan, Angela Browner
  • 2nd - Christina Aguilera Wendy Whitehurst
  • 3rd - Britney Spears, Faith Grayson
  • 4th - Hilary Duff, Erin Greenlee
  • 5th - Mary-Kate Olsen, Tina Blackmun
