Memorial Day Speakers
Honorary speakers highlight holiday commemorations.
May 27, 2002
By clue 5, three of the Memorial Day observance speakers in Washington County are Claymore, the person speaking at Veterans' Cemetery, and the one offering words in Linda Vista. By clue 10, the Veterans' Cemetery isn't in Summerset; nor is Claymore the Memorial Day speaker in Summerset (15). Therefore, the person speaking in Summerset is the fourth guest orator to the three named in clue 5. By clue 2, Lincoln isn't the speaker at the Linda Vista observance; nor is Lincoln the speaker at the Veterans' Cemetery or in Summerset (10). Lincoln is the fifth speaker to the four already noted. By clue 3, the speaker at the commemorations in Fallbrook is Sherman; the Fallbrook ceremonies must be at the Veterans' Cemetery. The speakers in Linda Vista and Summerset must be Gatling and Hatcher in some order. The Medal of Honor winner isn't the guest speaker in Linda Vista or Summerset (7) or at the Veterans' Cemetery (14) and isn't Claymore (7); the Medal of Honor winner is Lincoln. By clues 9 and 12, neither Hatcher nor Gatling is either the U. S. Rep. or Lt. Gen. (ret.), so Claymore and Sherman are in some order. Sherman is the Lt. Gen. (ret.) (14) and Claymore the U. S. Rep. Roger isn't Gatling (6) or Hatcher (9) and isn't the U. S. Rep. or Lt. Gen. (ret.) (9), so he is Lincoln. Jane isn't Lt. Gen. (ret.) Sherman, Gatling, or Hatcher (4); she is Rep. Claymore. Rep. Claymore isn't speaking in Moro Bay (8); she is at the remembrances in Grove City, and Sgt. Roger Lincoln is in Moro Bay. Sgt. Roger Lincoln isn't the speaker at the fairgrounds (19), nor is Rep. Jane Claymore (13); by clue 13, then, the fairgrounds are in Linda Vista. Neither Roger (1) nor Rep. Jane Claymore (8) is the speaker at the celebration at the Washington County courthouse, which then must be in Summerset. Brenda isn't the speaker in Linda Vista or at the courthouse in Summerset (11), so she is Lt. Gen. (ret) Sherman. By clue 16, the state Sen. is doing the honors at the courthouse, with the county commissioner speaking at the fairgrounds. The county commissioner is Donald (17) and the state Sen. Henry. Donald is Gatling and Henry Hatcher (20). Finally, by clue 18, Sgt. Lincoln is speaking at Battery Park in Moro Bay and Rep. Claymore at the Civil War monument in Grove City. The Memorial Day observance speakers in Washington County are

  • U. S. Rep. Jane Claymore at the Civil War monument in Grove City
  • County Commissioner Donald Gatling at the fairgrounds in Linda Vista
  • State Sen. Henry Hatcher at the courthouse in Summerset
  • Medal of Honor winner Sgt. Roger Lincoln at Battery Park in Moro Bay
  • Lt. Gen. (ret.) Brenda Sherman at Veterans' Cemetery in Fallbrook
