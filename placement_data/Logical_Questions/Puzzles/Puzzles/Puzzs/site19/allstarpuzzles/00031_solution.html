A Collection of Villains
Collectibles starring Disney villains brighten Wendy's birthday.
October 21, 2002
Three of the gifts Wendy received for her birthday are given in clue 2: the one from her Aunt Aurora, the Captain Hook collectible, and the paperweight. By clue 4, Grandma Belle gave Wendy the Ursula collectible, which isn't the paperweight; the Ursula collectible is then the fourth gift to the three in clue 2. Wendy's new Cruella de Vil collectible isn't the paperweight and didn't come from her Aunt Aurora (clue 6) and must therefore be the fifth gift to the four already named above. By clue 3, Wendy's brother John's gift wasn't the Cruella de Vil collectible; nor did he give her the paperweight (8). John gave Wendy the Captain Hook collectible. The Evil Queen collectible didn't come from sister Jasmine (7) or Uncle Peter (9) and thus is Aunt Aurora's gift. The paperweight bears Maleficent's likeness, by elimination. Jasmine didn't give Wendy the Maleficent collectible (1), so Uncle Peter did; and Jasmine gave her sister the Cruella de Vil item. By clue 3, the figurine is neither the Cruella de Vil nor the Captain Hook (from John) collectible, nor is it the Evil Queen gift (5). The figurine of Ursula is from Grandma Belle. The Cruella de Vil collectible isn't the snow globe (1) or the poster (7) and is the pin. Finally, by clue 7, the poster is of Captain Hook; and the Evil Queen collectible is the snow globe. Wendy's birthday collection of villains is as follows:

  • Captain Hook poster from brother John
  • Cruella de Vil pin from sister Jasmine
  • Evil Queen snow globe from Aunt Aurora
  • Maleficent paperweight from Uncle Peter
  • Ursula figurine from Grandma Belle
