Letter Dice 6
Find the six letters on each die in this easier Logic Puzzle.
March 28, 2005
According to the introduction, each of the 24 faces of the Letter Dice has a different letter on it; the letters J and Z do not appear. Given the word HAIR, we arbitrarily assign H to die 1, A to die 2, I to die 3, and R to die 4. From CHAD, D isn't on die 1 or die 2; while from LIED, D isn't in die 3. D is on die 4, with C then on die 3 (CHAD). Given FAKE, E isn't on die 2 with A; and from LIED, E isn't on die 3 with I or die 4 with D. E is on die 1 and L then on die 2. K isn't on die 4 with R (PARK); so the K in FAKE is on die 3 and the F on die 4. The P in PARK is on die 1. O isn't on die 1 with E (SOME), nor on die 2 with L or die 3 with K (YOLK). O is on die 4. Y is on die 1 (YOLK). G isn't on die 1 (GUSH), die 2 (GLUT), or die 3 (WING) and is on die 4. U isn't on die 1 or die 4 (GUSH), nor on die 2 (GLUT). U is on die 3. Then T is on die 1 (GLUT) and S on die 2 (GUSH). From RUBY, B is on die 2. M is on die 3 (SOME). X is on die 3 (BOXY). N isn't on die 1 (VINE), nor on 3 or 4 (WING); N is on die 2. Then V is on die 4 (VINE) and W on die 1 (WING). Q is the sixth letter on die 2. In sum, the letters on the four dice of Letter Dice 6 (in order of recovery) are

  • HEPYTW
  • ALSBNQ
  • ICKUMX
  • RDFOGV
