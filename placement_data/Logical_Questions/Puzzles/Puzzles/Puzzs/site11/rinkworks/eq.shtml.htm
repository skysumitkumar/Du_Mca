Scrambled Equations

In each puzzle you are given a number that you must construct out of several other numbers. You do this by taking the numbers and performing addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division operations on them. Each number must be used in the calculations exactly once, and only the four operations listed above may be used. You may parenthesize your expression however you wish. Sometimes multiple solutions are possible -- occasionally, multiple solutions will be listed on the solutions page but often there will be only one.

If you have trouble solving one of the puzzles, the corresponding Hint link will help you.
