Word Fragments

To solve the puzzles below, you must think of an English word that contains each of the given word fragments, i.e., you must form a word by adding letters to the beginning and/or the end of the fragment. You may not add letters to the middle of the fragment, nor may you rearrange the letters given.

We have multiple solutions for some of the word fragments (although we omitted most alternate solutions that rely on variations of the same word); the number of solutions we found is given in parentheses after the Solution link. To solve the puzzles, you only need to find one word for each fragment.
