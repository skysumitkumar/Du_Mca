5x5 Anacrossagrams

Anacrossagrams are like miniature crossword puzzles, except the clues are words, and what you fill into the grid is an anagram of the clue. The trick is to figure out what order the letters go in. You may wish to print this page out and work on paper.

If you have trouble solving one of the puzzles, the corresponding Hint link will fill in some of the grid for you.
