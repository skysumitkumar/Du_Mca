Solution to the /geometry/shephard.piano problem

This problem is unsolved. A simple solution called the "Shephard piano" (or Hammersley sofa) has area pi/2 + 2/pi = 2.2074.., but this can be improved upon with local modifications. It is known that a maximum area exists, but not whether the shape achieving it is symmetric, smooth, or even unique. For some interesting pictures and calculations see: The Moving Sofa Constant

See also Problem G5 in Croft, Falconer, and Guy, _Unsolved Porblems in Geometry_, Springer-Verlag, 1991.
