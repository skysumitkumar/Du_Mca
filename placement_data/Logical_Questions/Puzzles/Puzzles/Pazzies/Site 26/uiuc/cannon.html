Cannon!

Keys
  • Left/Right - to decrease/increase bullet speed (or select with mouse)
  • Up/Down - to move cannon up and down (1 degree)
  • Page-Up/Page-Down - keys to move the cannon faster (5 degrees)
  • CONTROL - to shoot
  • b - for bazooka

  • s - to turn sound on or off

Rules

  • You have 50 bullets to shoot as many targets as possible
  • The further the target the higher the points
  • The slower the bullet speed, the higher the points, so try to use slower speeds when possible!
    It is easy to aim with faster bullets, but you will score fewer points

Score Chart

  1. 0 - 6,000: Hey, are you playing with your eyes closed?!
  2. 6,001 - 8,000: Beginner
  3. 8,001 - 9,500: Average
  4. 9,501 - 12,500: Good!
  5. 12,501 - 16,500: Experienced!
  6. 16,501 - 19,500: Wow, you're a Sharp Shooter!
  7. 19,501 and above: Super Duper Sharp Shooter!

Fire away!!
