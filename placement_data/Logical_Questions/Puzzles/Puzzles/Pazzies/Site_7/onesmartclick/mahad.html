Mahad (Raigad)

An ancient township on the bank of river Savitri on Mumbai-Goa National Highway. It shot into fame when Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar started agitation for water at the site of 'Chawdar Tale'.

A 'Kranti Stambh' has been erected in the memory of this agitaion.

Mahad MIDC area is the largest industrial area in Konkan. Don't miss to visit the Fort Raigad and Pandwas sculptures.

Excursions: Raigad fort 27, Pale caves 3, Hot water springs 5 kms.

Distances: Bombay 175, Pune 99 kms

It is at a distance of 100 kms. from Alibag.

The right time to visit: October - March
