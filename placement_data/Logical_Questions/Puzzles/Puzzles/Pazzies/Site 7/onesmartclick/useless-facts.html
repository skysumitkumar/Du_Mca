Some Useless Facts

  1. Most lipstick contain fish scales!
  2. A crocodile always grows new teeth to replace the old teeth!
  3. Porcupines float in water!
  4. A hummingbird weighs less than a penny!
  5. A cockroach can live several weeks with its head cut off - it dies from starvation!
  6. One in every 4 Americans has appeared on television!
  7. Thomas Edison, lightbulb inventor, was afraid of the dark!
  8. Marie and Pierre Curie refused to patent their process of making radium, declaring it belonged to the world – no one had the right to profit from it.
  9. The worlds oldest piece of chewing gum is over 9000 years old!
  10. The elephant is the only mammal that can't jump!
  11. Chewing gum while peeling onions will keep you from crying!
  12. Bats always turn left when exiting a cave!
  13. You can't tickle yourself.
  14. You can't trademark surnames.
  15. The only part of the body that has no blood supply is the cornea in the eye. It takes in oxygen directly from the air.
  16. In most watch advertisments the time displayed on the watch is 10:10 because then the arms frame the brand of the watch and make it look like it's smiling.
  17. Charlie Chaplin won third place in a Charlie Chaplin look alike contest.
  18. India never invaded any country in her last 10000 years of history.
  19. Walt Disney was afraid of mice.
  20. The word "listen" contains the same letters as the word "silent".
  21. Hummingbirds are the only animal that can fly backwards.
  22. It is impossible to lick your elbow.
  23. Insects consume 10% of the world's food supply every year.
  24. The term karaoke means "empty orchestra" in Japanese.
  25. More than 60 million people annually visit France, a country of 60 million people.
  26. All the planets in the solar system rotate anticlockwise, except Venus. It is the only planet that rotates clockwise.
  27. A snail can sleep for 3 years.
  28. Electricity doesn't move through a wire but through a field around the wire.
  29. The water we drink is three billion years old.
  30. Ants don't sleep.
  31. Camel milk does not curdle.
  32. The only domestic animal not mentioned in the Bible is the cat.
  33. There are more insects in one square mile of rural land than there are human beings on the entire earth.
