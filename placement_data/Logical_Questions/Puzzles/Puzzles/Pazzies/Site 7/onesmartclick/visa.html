Coming to USA - VISA FAQ


Thought of collecting information about VISA but when I came across This Page I changed my mind.

Whatever you are reading out here has been extracted from Syed Sujat's page. Things have been presented keeping students seeking for admission for higher studies in US. But you'll get good idea even you are going for work.

The main focus is on the following:

Things to be carried to the Consulate :

A list of all the things you are required to carry to the Consulate. These are required to be carried by anyone appearing for an interview, or even if mailing your application.

Formats of all Certificates and Legal Papers:

Formats of all the papers that you require to carry along with you.

Financial Matters:

This section deals with all the questions you have regarding Financial Matters, be it Private FDs, Bank FDs, SB Amount, Huge Passbook Entries or whatever.

VISA Form :

This section has all the info ranging from how to fill the VISA Form to solving all your queries about all the sections in the VISA Form OF-156. You can also get a printer-friendly copy here.

Questions asked during the Interview:

This section covers almost all the questions usually asked during the VISA Interview. He has also tried to put in the best replies according to him

VISA Experiences:

This section holds a few experiences of students who have appeared for their VISA Intreview.

The procedure at Consulate plus Tips & Tricks:

This section lists all the various things you need to keep in mind for the interview or while preparing for it. In short "DOs and DONTs" of the VISA Interview . Also deals with what you should do at the Consulate or before your interview.
