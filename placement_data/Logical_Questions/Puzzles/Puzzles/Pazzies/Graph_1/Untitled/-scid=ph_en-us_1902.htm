Office Community Most Valuable Professional Web Sites

•Who are the Office Community Most Valuable Professionals?
Microsoft Most Valuable Professionals (MVPs) are subject matter experts who are awarded yearly for their high level of involvement and willingness to share in the peer to peer online community. Ranging in age from teen to senior citizen, some MVPs are technical professionals while others work in such diverse occupations as teaching, the arts, public service, medicine, etc.
•Microsoft Valuable Professional Web Site
This Web site contains links to the different Web sites maintained by the Microsoft MVPs. On this site you can choose the application you are interested in viewing and locate an MVP site that may interest you.
