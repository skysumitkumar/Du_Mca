Here is Kyle DeWitt's explanation:
Considering the way the numbers are deliberately printed in block form, make a 3 x 5 grid where each block of the grid corresponds to a block of the number a b c Each letter will have a value. (ie. c+e+h+j+m =45 as they form the number 1). So the sum of a-m=104 (number d e 8) As 0 and 8 differ only by the "g block" and there sums differ by 8, g=8. As 3 and 9 differ only by the "d block" f g h and their sums differ by 4, d=4. 3 and 8 differ by the "d block" and the "i block", and their sums differ by 14. i j d=4 so i=10. 2 and 8 differ by the "e block" and the "i block", their sums differ by 16, and i=10 so e=6. 6 and 8 k l m differ only by the "e block" therefore 6 corresponds to "8"-e or 104-6=98.
