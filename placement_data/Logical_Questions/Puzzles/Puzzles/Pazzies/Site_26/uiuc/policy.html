In the past we used an outside ad company to display ads on our site. These ads may have contained cookies. Cookies received with banner ads were collected by our ad company, and we do not have access to this information.

Contests

We run contests on our site (if you are one of the first three persons to respond correctly to a puzzle, your name is posted on the website) in which we ask visitors for contact information (like email address and name).
