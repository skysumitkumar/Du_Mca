Puzzle 1

If Poland is 44 miles away, Canada is 42 miles away and Mongolia is 56 miles away, how far away is Peru?

   Ref: ZEVZ © Kevin Stone

28: each vowel is worth 6 and each consonant is worth 8.

Puzzle 2

Can you find a five digit number that, if you place a 9 at the beginning to make a new number, is fours times larger than if you place the 9 at the end instead?

   Ref: ZCDE © Kevin Stone

23076: 923076 / 230769 = 4.

Puzzle 3

What comes next in this sequence:

fawn bear lion mole ==?==

Select from: seal duck stag vole wolf.

   Ref: ZQSQ © Kevin Stone

Duck: the second letters of each word are the vowels in alphabetical order, fAwn, bEar, lIon, mOle, dUck.

Puzzle 4

The contest consisted of a binary code transmission where the spaces between the letters were missing and there was no punctuation. Each letter of the alphabet was translated into its binary equivalent based on its position in the alphabet, a=1, b=10, c=11, d=100, e=101, f=110, g=111, h=1000, i=1001, j=1010, k=1011, l=1100, m=1101, n=1110, o=1111, p=10000, q=10001, r=10010, s=10011, t=10100, u=10101, v=10110, w=10111, x=11000, y=11001, z=11010. Can you find 10 countries?

101011000011110
101111110010110011
100001111110011110100
110100101111011101
11100010011100101
1101111110010111111111111
11010111011010011
10100110011011111110
1000011110111011
1101111111101111111110010011

   Ref: ZGZM © Kevin Stone

Japan, Wales, Poland, France, Chile, Morocco, Zambia, Taiwan, Panama, Mongolia.

Puzzle 5

som one dbr pen rtm ber
nes ent ive eph eti ens
mes off eak eid sep win
erd tel day ter wed tem
aft apa own car ern oon
