Random Puzzles

Puzzle 1

Can you find a five digit number which has no zeros nor ones in it and no digit is repeated, where:

The fourth digit is a quarter of the total of all of the digits.
The second digit is twice the first digit.
The third digit is the largest.
The last digit is the sum of the first two digits.

   Ref: ZPXQ © Kevin Stone

24976: remember the question stated that the number contained no zeros nor ones.

Puzzle 4

Starting with BALL, change one letter at a time until you have the word HEAD. Each change must result in a proper word. What is the minimum number of steps required to achieve this change?

   Ref: ZCOG © Kevin Stone

Ball bell hell heal head.
Ball bell hell held head.
Ball hall hell heal head.
Ball hall hell held head.
