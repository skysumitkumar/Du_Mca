Solution

Question:
The Mayor of a city C couldn't help but notice that a large population traveled from point A to point B and back everyday. Moreover, there was no straightforward route, and the commute was long and winding. The Mayor thought it would be a great idea to build an expressway from point A to point B. This would drastically reduce the time for the commute. To the Mayor's surprise, after the expressway was opened, none of these people traveled from point A to B any more.

What was the reason?

Answer:

  1. CMontgomery
    People were actually going to point B to see the city's famous Snowy Owl preserve. The noise of building the expressway, however, combined with the removal of several trees to make room for the route (amidst much protest by the local wildlife community), frightened off the owls, thus removing the desire to travel to point B.

  2. RMontgomery
    There was a strip mine at B, and many people living at A worked there. About the time the Mayor completed his road the ore ran out, and nobody wanted to go to an abandoned strip-mine.

  3. Cynthia W. Munsell
    Before the expressway was built, the mail, because of the length of the journey from A to B, although it was actually in the same city, took several days, so instead of using the postal service, many people delivered their own mail, or hired couriers to travel the route for them. After the expressway was built, the postal service started using a route which utilized the expressway, and so the couriers and personal deliveries were no longer needed.

  4. Rudy Hice
    After the expressway was made they were able to go from A to C instead of A to B to C.
    Comment: This is an interesting solution, however the Mayor would have noticed that the people were traveling from A to C.

  5. Greg Jones
    The business located at point 'B' was aware that it was a long winding commute from 'A' to 'B', and would allow the time for the commute to be part of an employee's 'paid' workday, as well as compensating for gas and millage. With the new expressway, the business no longer compensated the employees. Without the extra comp, the employees decided to find other employment.
