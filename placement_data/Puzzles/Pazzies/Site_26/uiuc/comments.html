Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Q: Do you guys actively maintain or update this site with new puzzles?

    A: Not actively. We feel like we have made a sizable contribution where newcomers can spend months trying to solve our puzzles.

  2. Q: What happened to your Weekly Coolpuzzle?

    A: After a while it became a lot harder to find coolpuzzles. To maintain the quality of puzzles on this site, we discontinued the Weekly Coolpuzzle. You might want to check out this Monthly Puzzle site.

  3. Q: I cannot see any of the games. Either I see gray rectangles, or I get an "Applet not started" error, or I see nothing at all! What's the problem?

    A: Our Java Applets are compatible only with newer versions of Netscape or Internet Explorer. We generally suggest Netscape 4.6 or better or Internet Explorer 4.x or 5.x. If you believe that you have a capable browser, then maybe you have not "Enabled Java" in your preferences. To find out how to Enable Java in your browser please refer to the following link: http://www.chami.com/tips/internet/020597I.html

  4. Q: Where are the answers to all the puzzles?

    A: The puzzles that have not been solved have a * next to their titles. The solutions to these are not put up until we have 3 correct responses. For all other puzzles, you will see something like, "Solved by: Apu, Samarth & Joe." Just click on the word "Solved", which is linked to the solution.

  5. Q:Why do some puzzles have their solutions up with less than 3 peoples' names?

    A: These are most likely older weekly cool puzzles. Since we have a policy that weekly cool puzzle solutions will be put up within one week, we have to put up the solutions even if there are lesser than 3 correct responses.
