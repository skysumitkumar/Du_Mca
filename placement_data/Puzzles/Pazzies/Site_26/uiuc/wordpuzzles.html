  3. Ice Cream (Novice)

    Dakotah and Cassie are outside an ice cream shop. Cassie tells Dakotah, "I will buy you an ice cream cone if you tell me the answer to this question: How is half of 5, 4?" Dakotah thinks about this for a minute and then Cassie buys him and ice cream. What did Dakotah say?

    Submitted by: Dako G. Thanks!
    Solved by: The Aikens, Mike Kuppersmith & WAFA

  4. QWERTY (Intermediate)

    What is the longest word in the English language that can be spelled by using only left hand side of a QWERTY keyboard?

    Note: the boundary for the left hand side is T, G and V (all included). The QWERTY keyboard is the standard keyboard where the first 6 keys on the top row from left to right are q,w,e,r,t and y.

    Submitted by: Dako G. Thanks!
    Solved by: Mike Dickheiser, Ryan Plesniarski & Brian Cornish

  13. Now try an english sentence with five consecutive ands. (Expert)
    Solution

Word to Word

You need to figure out a set of valid english words that will transform the first word into the second word. Each consecutive word has to be formed by changing one, and only one, letter from the previous word. E.g., a solution to wire->...->foam would be: wire->fire->fore->form->foam. Of course, there could be various other solutions! If in doubt, we will verify your words through Merriam Webster Online. We encourage you to check your words there first.
