Question:
Two Tiered Sequence (Master)

Complete the next 8 numbers of the following sequence. That is, you need to place the numbers 18-25 correctly, above or below the line. You also need to explain your answer.

1,5,6,8,9,11,12,13,14
---------------------
2,3,4,7,10,15,16,17

Solved by: Jennifer Reaves, Dan Higgins & Jon Lalla

Answer:
The trick is to first replace the numbers by their corresponding letters. So here is the transformed sequence:

A     E,F, H,I, K,L,M,N
--------------------------
 B,C,D,  G,  J,      O,P,Q
If you notice, all letters that can be written with straight lines are above the line, and all letters that are written with at least one curve are below the line! So the numbers 18-25, that correspond to the letters R,S,T,U,V,W,X and Y go as follows: 
   T, V,W,X,Y
----------------
R,S, U

20,22,23,24,25
----------------
18,19,21
So here is the completed sequence: 
1,5,6,8,9,11,12,13,14,20,22,23,24,25
------------------------------------
2,3,4,7,10,15,16,17,18,19,21
