Indian-American English


Mind Your Language

Speak slowly and pronounce properly.The first part you really need to practice otherwise you'll hear "come again", "pardon me", "I'm sorry", "I didn't get you etc." from the person opposite. Be ready for the changes in pronounciation like (Schedule -> skedule),(Route -> raut), And substitutions like (Petrol -> gas) etc.
