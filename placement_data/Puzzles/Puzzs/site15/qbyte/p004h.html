Hint to puzzle 4: Equatorial belt

  1. Write an equation connecting d, the extra belt length; r, the radius of the Earth; 2a, the length of the belt not in contact with the Earth; and x, the angle subtended at the center of the Earth.
  2. Draw a radius from the center of the Earth to one of the points of contact between the Earth and the belt.  What angle is made between the radius and the belt?
