Weigh group 2 against group 3.

If the groups are in equilibroum, then
  • The false coin is one of 3 coins interchanged at step (A).
  • The false coin is lighter then the good coins.
If the plates switch positions, then
  • The false coin is one of the coins interchanged at step (B).
  • It is not known if the false coin is heavier or lighter then the good coin.
If the plates are in the previous position, then
  • the false coin is among the remaining three heavy coins
  • The false coin is heavier then the good coins.
